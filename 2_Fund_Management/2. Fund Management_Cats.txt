2. Fund Management Contracts

2-1X_Mutual Fund Token Contracts: Smart contracts to tokenize mutual funds, enabling fractional ownership and streamlined trading.

2-1Y_Hedge Fund Token Contracts: Contracts designed for hedge funds, including mechanisms for managing investments, performance fees, and profit distribution.

2-1Z_Exchange-Traded Fund (ETF) Contracts: Smart contracts for creating and managing tokenized ETFs that can be traded on digital exchanges.

------------------------------

2-1X_Mutual Fund Token Contracts: Smart contracts to tokenize mutual funds, enabling fractional ownership and streamlined trading.

2-1X_### **1. ERC1400: Security Tokens Standard**
ERC1400 is ideal for tokenizing mutual funds, ensuring compliance with regulatory frameworks while allowing for fractional ownership of fund assets.

2-1X_1A - **Mutual Fund Token Issuance Contract**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Facilitates the issuance of tokens representing fractional ownership of a mutual fund. These tokens comply with regulatory requirements, ensuring that the issuance process adheres to securities laws.

2-1X_1B - **Transfer Restrictions Contract for Mutual Funds**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Implements regulatory and compliance restrictions on mutual fund token transfers. Only accredited or compliant investors can trade or transfer their fractional shares of the mutual fund.

2-1X_1C - **Dividend and Yield Distribution Contract**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Automatically distributes dividends or yield generated from the mutual fund’s portfolio to token holders based on their fractional ownership. This streamlines the process of profit distribution.

2-1X_1D - **Lock-Up Period Contract for Mutual Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Enforces lock-up periods during which investors cannot sell or transfer their mutual fund tokens, ensuring compliance with regulatory or fund-specific policies for early investors or certain investment rounds.

2-1X_1E - **Redemption Contract for Mutual Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Allows investors to redeem their mutual fund tokens for their proportional share of the underlying assets or equivalent value, providing liquidity options for mutual fund token holders.

2-1X_1F - **Whitelisting/Blacklisting Contract for Mutual Funds**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Ensures that only verified and compliant investors (whitelisted addresses) can hold or trade mutual fund tokens, while blacklisting unauthorized participants.

---

2-1X_### **2. ERC1404: Restricted Token Standard**
ERC1404 focuses on tokenizing mutual funds with built-in transfer restrictions based on regulatory compliance requirements.

2-1X_2A - **Restricted Mutual Fund Token Contract**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1404  
  **Use Case**: Tokenizes mutual fund ownership with transfer restrictions to ensure that only compliant investors can hold and trade the tokens. This is especially important for private or restricted investment funds.

2-1X_2B - **Investor Verification Contract for Mutual Funds**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1404  
  **Use Case**: Verifies whether token holders are accredited or meet regulatory requirements, ensuring that only qualified investors can participate in the mutual fund. Ideal for funds with restrictions on investor types.

2-1X_2C - **Compliance Reporting Contract for Mutual Funds**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1404  
  **Use Case**: Automatically generates reports to regulatory authorities, demonstrating that all token transfers and holdings comply with relevant financial regulations for mutual funds.

2-1X_2D - **Tax Withholding Contract for Mutual Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1404  
  **Use Case**: Automatically calculates and withholds taxes on mutual fund token dividends, distributions, or transactions, ensuring that investors and the fund comply with tax regulations.

---

2-1X_### **3. ERC20: Fungible Token Standard**
ERC20 is a suitable standard for tokenizing mutual funds, allowing for fractional ownership and ease of transfer among investors.

2-1X_3A - **Fungible Mutual Fund Token Contract**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC20  
  **Use Case**: Tokenizes fractional ownership of a mutual fund, with each ERC20 token representing a portion of the fund’s assets. Investors can easily trade or transfer these tokens on the open market, providing liquidity.

2-1X_3B - **Dividend Distribution for ERC20 Mutual Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC20  
  **Use Case**: Automates the distribution of dividends or yield generated by the mutual fund’s portfolio to ERC20 token holders, based on their proportional ownership of the fund.

2-1X_3C - **Minting and Burning Contract for Mutual Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC20  
  **Use Case**: Manages the minting of new mutual fund tokens when additional assets are added to the fund and the burning of tokens when assets are removed or redeemed. Ensures the correct representation of fund assets over time.

2-1X_3D - **Fund Governance Contract for ERC20 Mutual Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC20  
  **Use Case**: Provides a decentralized governance structure for mutual fund investors, allowing token holders to vote on key fund decisions, such as asset allocation or changes in fund management.

---

2-1X_### **4. ERC777: Advanced Fungible Token Standard**
ERC777 enhances mutual fund token functionalities by offering greater control and flexibility over token operations.

2-1X_4A - **Advanced Mutual Fund Token Contract**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC777  
  **Use Case**: Issues mutual fund tokens with enhanced features like operator permissions, allowing fund managers or custodians to execute transactions or manage assets on behalf of token holders.

2-1X_4B - **Operator-Controlled Redemption Contract for Mutual Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC777  
  **Use Case**: Allows authorized operators (such as fund managers) to redeem mutual fund tokens for underlying assets or liquidity on behalf of token holders, ensuring efficient liquidity management within the fund.

2-1X_4C - **Dividend and Profit Sharing Contract for ERC777 Mutual Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC777  
  **Use Case**: Automates the distribution of profits and dividends to ERC777 token holders, with added flexibility for fund managers to control and authorize transactions.

---

2-1X_### **5. ERC1155: Multi-Token Standard**
ERC1155 allows for the tokenization of multiple types of assets, making it ideal for mutual funds with diverse portfolios.

2-1X_5A - **Multi-Asset Mutual Fund Token Contract**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1155  
  **Use Case**: Tokenizes a mutual fund that holds multiple types of assets (e.g., stocks, bonds, commodities). Each token within the contract can represent a fractional share of different asset classes within the fund, allowing for diversified ownership.

2-1X_5B - **Batch Transfer Contract for Mutual Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1155  
  **Use Case**: Enables the transfer of multiple types of mutual fund tokens in a single transaction, reducing gas costs and improving efficiency for investors trading diversified fund shares.

---

2-1X_### **6. ERC4626: Tokenized Vault Standard**
ERC4626 is specifically designed for tokenizing vaults, making it highly suitable for mutual funds that act as pooled investment vehicles.

2-1X_6A - **Mutual Fund Vault Token Contract**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC4626  
  **Use Case**: Tokenizes a mutual fund as a vault, where each token represents a share in the pooled assets managed by the fund. Investors can buy and sell vault tokens, which represent their ownership in the underlying assets of the mutual fund.

2-1X_6B - **Staking and Yield Contract for Mutual Fund Vaults**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC4626  
  **Use Case**: Allows investors to stake their mutual fund vault tokens and earn yield based on the performance of the underlying assets, providing a way for token holders to earn returns without directly trading their tokens.

---

2-1X_### **7. ERC998: Composable Non-Fungible Token Standard**
ERC998 enables composable mutual fund tokens, which can represent multiple assets within a single token.

2-1X_7A - **Composable Mutual Fund Token Contract**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC998  
  **Use Case**: Tokenizes a mutual fund where each token can represent ownership of a basket of different assets, such as stocks, bonds, or real estate. This composability allows investors to manage a diversified portfolio within a single token.

2-1X_7B - **Composable Asset Bundling Contract**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC998  
  **Use Case**: Bundles multiple mutual fund tokens or assets into a single composable token, making it easier for investors to manage and trade multiple assets within a mutual fund structure.

---

2-1X_### **8. AnonCreds: Privacy-Preserving Credentials**
AnonCreds provides privacy-preserving accreditation for mutual fund investors, ensuring compliance without revealing personal data.

2-1X_8A - **Accredited Investor Verification for Mutual Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: AnonCreds (Privacy-Preserving Credentials)  
  **Use Case**: Verifies the accreditation status of mutual fund token holders without revealing sensitive personal information. This ensures that only qualified investors can participate in tokenized mutual funds, maintaining compliance with privacy and regulatory requirements.

---

This comprehensive list covers the main types of **Mutual Fund Token Contracts** for fractional ownership and streamlined trading. Each ERC standard provides unique features that support the efficient and compliant operation of tokenized mutual funds, ensuring that the system is scalable, secure, and user-friendly.

2-1Y_Hedge Fund Token Contracts: Contracts designed for hedge funds, including mechanisms for managing investments, performance fees, and profit distribution.

2-1Y_### **1. ERC1400: Security Tokens Standard**
ERC1400 is well-suited for hedge fund tokenization, ensuring compliance with securities regulations and providing flexible governance.

2-1Y_1A - **Hedge Fund Token Issuance Contract**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Facilitates the issuance of hedge fund tokens, representing fractional ownership of the fund’s assets. These tokens comply with securities regulations, allowing hedge funds to raise capital and manage investor ownership digitally.

2-1Y_1B - **Performance Fee Contract for Hedge Funds**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Automatically calculates and distributes performance fees to fund managers based on the hedge fund’s performance. Typically, performance fees are a percentage of the fund’s profits that exceed a predefined threshold.

2-1Y_1C - **Profit Distribution Contract for Hedge Funds**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Automatically distributes profits or dividends generated from the hedge fund’s portfolio to token holders based on their fractional ownership. Ensures transparent and accurate distribution of returns to investors.

2-1Y_1D - **Transfer Restrictions Contract for Hedge Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400

2-1Y_1E - **Lock-Up Period Contract for Hedge Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Enforces lock-up periods on hedge fund tokens, restricting their transfer or sale for a predefined period after issuance. This is typically used to prevent early investors from exiting the fund prematurely.

2-1Y_1F - **Redemption Contract for Hedge Fund Tokens**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Allows investors to redeem their hedge fund tokens for their share of the fund’s assets or cash value. This contract provides a structured exit mechanism for investors wishing to withdraw their investments.

2-1Y_1G - **Whitelisting/Blacklisting Contract for Hedge Fund Investors**  
  **ERC Standard**: ERC1400  
  **Use Case**: Ensures that only authorized or whitelisted investors can hold or transfer hedge fund tokens, while blacklisting unauthorized participants.
